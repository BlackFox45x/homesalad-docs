Game Concept

Core Concept

HomeSalad is a community-building RPG with gardening mechanics and environmental activism themes. Players transform a struggling town through sustainable practices, progressing from newcomer to community leader and environmental champion.

Unique Selling Points

  • Community Transformation: Visible evolution of environment and social structures
  • Real-World Knowledge: Practical sustainability techniques applicable to real life
  • Level 1-60 Progression: Comprehensive character and community development
  • Dynamic Seasons System: Seasonal gameplay affecting all systems
  • Faction System: Complex relationships with various community groups

Setting: Greenville

An agricultural community that fell on hard times after manufacturing decline, now facing economic hardship, environmental degradation, and corporate takeover threats.

Core Themes

  • Environmental Action: Balancing immediate needs with long-term sustainability
  • Community Building: Creating resilient social structures and relationships
  • Knowledge Transfer: Preserving and sharing traditional and innovative practices
  • Resource Management: Strategic use of limited resources for maximum impact
  • Corporate Resistance: Opposing exploitative economic models

Environmental Transformation

A core feature of HomeSalad is the visible transformation of the environment as players restore ecological health.

Degraded

Polluted, barren, and struggling environments

Transitional

Partially restored environments showing signs of recovery

Restored

Thriving, biodiverse, and resilient environments

The game's environmental system tracks the state of each area on a scale from 0 (fully degraded) to 10 (fully restored). Player actions, plant growth, and sustainable infrastructure all contribute to environmental improvement.
