HomeSalad Story Bible

This Story Bible provides the complete narrative foundation for HomeSalad, detailing the world, characters, factions, locations, and cultural elements that make up the game's rich storytelling environment.

Introduction

HomeSalad is set in Greenville, a once-thriving agricultural community that fell on hard times after the decline of its manufacturing sector. Now facing economic hardship, environmental degradation, and the threat of corporate takeover by MegaGrow Industries, the town stands at a crossroads.

The player arrives as a newcomer with skills in sustainability and community building, joining the fragmented Green Spark Collective to revitalize the community through gardening, sustainable technology, and grassroots organizing. The narrative follows their journey from newcomer to community leader and environmental champion.

This Story Bible documents the complete world lore, narrative structure, character profiles, faction descriptions, locations, cultural elements, and environmental systems that form the foundation of HomeSalad's storytelling.

Narrative Themes

HomeSalad explores several interconnected themes through its narrative:

Environmental Action

The story explores the tension between immediate needs and long-term sustainability, showing how environmental restoration can be integrated with community well-being rather than positioned as a luxury or distant concern.

Community Building

The narrative demonstrates how resilient social structures and relationships form the foundation of sustainable communities, emphasizing cooperation, knowledge sharing, and mutual support.

Knowledge Transfer

The game highlights the importance of preserving traditional practices while embracing innovation, creating a dialogue between generations and cultures about sustainable living.

Resource Management

The story shows the strategic use of limited resources for maximum impact, illustrating principles of circular economy, waste reduction, and regenerative design.

Corporate Resistance

The conflict with MegaGrow Industries explores alternatives to exploitative economic models, questioning the assumption that corporate-led development is the only path to prosperity.

Hopeful Realism

The overall tone acknowledges real challenges while emphasizing the possibility of positive change, avoiding both naive optimism and paralyzing despair.

Story-Gameplay Integration

The narrative of HomeSalad is deeply integrated with its gameplay systems:

  • Environmental Transformation: The visual evolution of Greenville from degraded to restored environments directly reflects player progress and serves as both storytelling and feedback.
  • Character Relationships: The development of relationships with NPCs unlocks new narrative content, resources, and gameplay opportunities.
  • Faction Standing: The player's reputation with different organizations affects available quests, dialogue options, and story branches.
  • Seasonal Storytelling: The changing seasons drive both gameplay mechanics and narrative pacing, with events and story beats tied to the natural cycle.
  • Knowledge as Progression: Learning new techniques and information serves both as character progression and narrative development.
