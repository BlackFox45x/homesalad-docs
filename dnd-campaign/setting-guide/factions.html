Factions of Verdantis

Discover the organizations and groups that shape the political landscape of Verdantis, from those working to restore the land to those exploiting its resources.

Factions Overview

The world of Verdantis is shaped by the ongoing conflict between those who seek to restore balance to the natural world and those who exploit it for power and profit. Various factions vie for influence, each with their own vision for the future of this magical realm.

Connection to HomeSalad

The factions of Verdantis are fantasy reflections of the organizations found in the HomeSalad video game. While reimagined through a magical lens, they maintain the same core values, conflicts, and themes.

View the original HomeSalad factions documentation to see how these fantasy organizations parallel their modern counterparts.

Major Factions

The Verdant Covenant

Leader: Willow Greenmantle (High Druid)

Headquarters: The Elder Grove (Verdant Heart)

Alignment: Neutral Good

The Verdant Covenant is an alliance of druids, rangers, nature-oriented clerics, and common folk dedicated to restoring the magical balance of Verdantis. They operate through a decentralized network of circles, each focused on a different aspect of environmental restoration.

HomeSalad Parallel:

The Verdant Covenant is the fantasy counterpart to The Green Spark Collective from HomeSalad. Both organizations focus on grassroots environmentalism, community building, and sustainable practices.

The Blightbound Consortium

Leader: Lord Malachor (Archmage)

Headquarters: Blightspire (Blighted Fens)

Alignment: Lawful Evil

The Blightbound Consortium is a powerful alliance of necromancers, corrupt merchants, and power-hungry nobles who see magical corruption not as a crisis but as an opportunity. They have developed methods to harness corrupted magic for personal gain, regardless of the environmental cost.

HomeSalad Parallel:

The Blightbound Consortium is the fantasy counterpart to MegaGrow Industries from HomeSalad. Both organizations prioritize profit and control over environmental health and community autonomy.

The Council of Verdantis

Leader: Elder Elyndra (Council Chair)

Headquarters: Greenhollow (Verdant Heart)

Alignment: True Neutral

The Council of Verdantis represents the formal governing body for the major settlements across the realm. Currently, the Council is deeply divided between those who favor the Verdant Covenant's approach to restoration and those who are swayed by the Blightbound Consortium's promises of prosperity and power.

HomeSalad Parallel:

The Council of Verdantis is the fantasy counterpart to the Greenville City Council from HomeSalad. Both serve as battlegrounds where competing visions for the future clash in the political arena.

Minor Factions

Beyond the major powers, several smaller factions operate throughout Verdantis, each with their own specialized focus and regional influence:

The Elderwood Keepers

An ancient order of treants, dryads, and elven druids who preserve traditional knowledge about the land and its magic.

HomeSalad Parallel: Riverside Elders Association

The Ironheart Salvagers

A guild of dwarves, gnomes, and human craftspeople who specialize in reclaiming and repurposing abandoned mines and magical machinery.

HomeSalad Parallel: Rust Belt Reclamation Crew

The Azure Current

An alliance of water genasi, tritons, and coastal communities dedicated to protecting and restoring the waters of Verdantis.

HomeSalad Parallel: Brightwater Environmental Coalition

The Greenhollow Exchange

A merchant guild that promotes sustainable trade throughout Verdantis, specializing in ethically sourced magical components.

HomeSalad Parallel: Downtown Business Alliance

Faction Relationships & Community Bond Network

Player interactions with factions are tracked through the Community Bond Network, a mechanical system that measures your party's relationship with various groups. As you complete quests, make decisions, and interact with faction members, your bond level will change, affecting the benefits and opportunities available to you.

Connection to HomeSalad

The Community Bond Network is the fantasy counterpart to the Faction Reputation System in HomeSalad. Both systems track player relationships with organizations and provide mechanical benefits for building these connections.

For more detailed information on faction relationships and how to use them in your campaign, see the Core Rules and Starter Adventure.
