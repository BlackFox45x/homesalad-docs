Welcome to Homesalid: Realms of the Forgotten Path

Deep within the mists of time lies Verdantis, the fantasy reflection of Greenville from the HomeSalad universe. Once a vibrant realm where nature and civilization existed in perfect harmony, Verdantis now faces a growing crisis as magical corruption spreads across the land, mirroring the environmental challenges of our own world.

This campaign setting provides everything you need to run epic adventures that blend classic D&D fantasy with meaningful environmental and community themes:

  • A rich, detailed world where environmental health directly affects magical potency
  • Compelling factions working to either restore or exploit the natural world
  • Unique character options including the Restoration Druid, Guardian Ranger, and Community Bard
  • A complete campaign arc from levels 1-20 focused on healing a wounded world
  • An innovative Environmental State System that tracks your impact on the world
  • The Community Bond Network for building meaningful relationships with NPCs and settlements
"In Verdantis, the land itself remembers both kindness and cruelty. Every seed planted, every tree saved, every stream cleansed strengthens the world's magic. Every resource exploited without care weakens the veil between order and chaos." — Elderoak, Ancient Guardian of the Verdant Heart

Campaign Features

Environmental Restoration

The core conflict of this campaign centers on the magical corruption spreading throughout Verdantis, a direct parallel to environmental degradation in the HomeSalad universe. As heroes, your primary quest is to heal the land and restore balance to nature.

Using the Environmental State System, your actions directly impact the health of different regions. Restore a corrupted forest, cleanse a polluted river, or reawaken dormant ley lines to see the world transform before your eyes—from barren wastelands to vibrant, magical ecosystems.

Community Bonds

True change requires more than individual heroism—it demands community action. The Community Bond Network allows you to forge meaningful relationships with settlements and NPCs throughout Verdantis.

As you help villages implement sustainable practices, teach them to coexist with magical creatures, and defend them from those who would exploit the land, you'll build a network of allies committed to environmental restoration. These bonds provide mechanical benefits while creating a rich, interconnected narrative.

Unique Character Options

Create heroes specifically equipped to address the challenges of Verdantis with new subclasses like the Restoration Druid, Guardian Ranger, Geomancer Wizard, and Community Bard.

New backgrounds such as Nature Warden, Village Healer, and Reclaimer tie your character directly to the environmental themes of the campaign, providing both narrative hooks and mechanical benefits focused on restoration and community-building.

Connection to HomeSalad

This campaign serves as the fantasy reflection of the HomeSalad universe, where Verdantis represents Greenville through a magical lens. The environmental challenges and community dynamics mirror those found in the core HomeSalad narrative.

Throughout the campaign, players will discover subtle connections to the HomeSalad universe—familiar locations reimagined in fantasy form, parallel characters with similar motivations, and themes that resonate across both worlds. These connections enrich the story while maintaining a distinct fantasy experience.

Getting Started

Ready to begin your adventure in Verdantis and make a difference in this magical world? Here's how to get started:

  1. Explore Verdantis - Familiarize yourself with this unique fantasy world in the World Overview section.
  2. Learn the Systems - Understand the Core Rules, including the Environmental State System and Community Bond Network.
  3. Create Characters - Use the Character Creation guide to build heroes committed to restoring balance to the natural world.
  4. Prepare for Adventure - Dungeon Masters should review the Starter Adventure, "Echoes of the Forgotten," which introduces the core themes of environmental restoration.
  5. Begin the Journey - Gather your party and venture forth to heal a wounded world!
