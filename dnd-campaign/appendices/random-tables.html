Random Tables

Generate encounters, treasures, NPCs, and more for your adventures in the Realms of the Forgotten Path.

Using Random Tables

Random tables are powerful tools for Dungeon Masters, providing inspiration and unexpected elements that can breathe life into your adventures. The tables in this section are designed specifically for the Homesalid setting, incorporating its unique features, themes, and creatures.

Regional Encounter Tables

These tables provide region-specific encounters for the major areas of Verdantis. Roll a d20 to determine what the characters encounter while traveling through these regions.

The Verdant Heart
d20 Encounter
1 A patrol of 1d4+2 Verdant Covenant rangers monitoring the forest's health
2 A circle of druids performing a ritual to strengthen a local ley line
3 2d4 forest animals behaving unusually, showing signs of intelligence
4 A wandering treant tending to younger trees and plants
5 A small shrine to nature spirits with fresh offerings of seeds and clear water
6-20 Additional encounters (to be expanded in future updates)
The Blighted Fens
d20 Encounter
1 A thick patch of mist that causes minor hallucinations of lost memories
2 1d4 corrupted mistwalkers stalking the party
3 A local guide offering services (50% chance of being an Arcane Exploitation League spy)
4 2d4 swamp zombies rising from the corrupted water
5 A witch's hut that seems to move locations each day, inhabited by a healer studying corruption
6-20 Additional encounters (to be expanded in future updates)

Environmental State Encounter Modifiers

The Environmental State of a region affects the types of encounters that occur there. Use these tables to modify regional encounters based on the current Environmental State.

Pristine (Environmental State 9-10)
d8 Additional Encounter Element
1 A nature spirit offers a blessing (+1d4 to nature-related checks for 24 hours)
2 Vibrant magical flora provides ingredients for potions of greater healing
3 A rare magical creature approaches the party with curiosity rather than fear
4 Natural healing is enhanced (recover 1 additional hit die during a short rest)
5-8 Additional effects (to be expanded in future updates)
Critical (Environmental State 0-2)
d8 Additional Encounter Element
1 Corruption exposure (DC 15 Constitution save or take 1d6 poison damage)
2 Spellcasting disruption (DC 15 Constitution check when casting or spell fails)
3 1d4 corrupted animals attack the party
4 Wild magic surge (roll on the Wild Magic Surge table when any spell is cast)
5-8 Additional effects (to be expanded in future updates)

Environmental Change Tables

These tables help Dungeon Masters track and generate environmental changes that occur as a result of player actions or external factors.

Environmental Restoration Effects
d12 Visible Effect of Successful Restoration
1 Withered plants begin to show new growth within days
2 Water sources become clearer, with natural aquatic life returning
3 Birdsong returns to previously silent areas
4 Soil regains its natural color and fertility
5-12 Additional effects (to be expanded in future updates)

Community Interaction Tables

Use these tables to generate interactions with communities based on their Bond Level with the party.

d8 Community Response (Bond Level 3-5)
1 The community holds a feast in the party's honor, sharing their best sustainable food
2 Local craftspeople offer to create custom items incorporating environmental themes
3 Community members volunteer to assist with restoration activities
4 The village elder shares ancient knowledge about local ley lines and nature spirits
