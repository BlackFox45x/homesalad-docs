How to Use These Character Sheets

  1. Choose the Right Sheet - Select the sheet that best fits your character and play style.
  2. Fill in Basic Information - Record your character's name, class, level, and background.
  3. Record Ability Scores - Enter your ability scores and note the corresponding modifiers.
  4. Add Skills and Proficiencies - Mark which skills and saving throws your character is proficient in.
  5. Note Special Features - Record class features, racial traits, and background abilities.
  6. Track Environmental Connections - Use the Environmental State System tracker to monitor your impact on different regions.
  7. Record Community Bonds - Track your relationships with settlements and NPCs in the Community Bond Network.
  8. Log Restoration Activities - Keep a record of all environmental restoration work your character performs.
  9. Note Environmental Effects - Record how different Environmental States affect your character's abilities.

Remember to update your character sheet after each session to reflect new items, abilities, experiences, and environmental impacts.

Environmental State System Integration

The Environmental State System is a core mechanic in the Homesalid campaign that measures the health of different regions on a scale from 0 (completely corrupted) to 10 (pristine and flourishing). Our character sheets include special sections for tracking this system:

Environmental State Effects

The character sheets include a reference table showing how different Environmental States affect your character:

Environmental State Effects on Character
0-2 (Critical)
  • Spellcasting requires DC 15 Constitution checks
  • Risk of corruption exposure (1d6 damage per day)
  • Disadvantage on nature-based checks
3-4 (Degraded)
  • Spellcasting requires DC 10 Constitution checks
  • Natural healing halved
  • No bonuses from environment-based abilities
5-6 (Stressed)
  • Minor penalties to nature-based skill checks
  • Normal spellcasting and healing
  • Reduced effectiveness of environmental magic
7-8 (Healthy)
  • Advantage on nature-based skill checks
  • Additional hit die recovery during long rests
  • Enhanced environmental magic
9-10 (Pristine)
  • Add proficiency bonus to spell damage/healing once per short rest
  • Temporary hit points after long rests
  • Possible nature spirit boons

Restoration Activity Tracking

The character sheets include a section for recording restoration activities:

  • Physical Restoration: Cleaning pollution, planting vegetation, creating wildlife habitats
  • Magical Purification: Cleansing corrupted magic, stabilizing ley lines, dispelling harmful effects
  • Spiritual Renewal: Reconnecting with nature spirits, performing renewal rituals
  • Knowledge Sharing: Teaching sustainable practices, researching ancient techniques

Each activity contributes to improving the Environmental State of a region, with multiple successful activities eventually raising the state by one point.

Community Bond Network Integration

The Community Bond Network tracks relationships between player characters and the communities they interact with throughout Verdantis. Our character sheets include specialized sections for tracking these bonds:

Bond Level Benefits

The character sheets include a reference table showing the benefits of different Bond Levels:

Bond Level Benefits
0 (Strangers) No special benefits; standard prices and treatment
1 (Acquaintances)
  • 10% discount on goods and services
  • Basic information sharing
  • Simple assistance with minor tasks
2 (Friends)
  • 20% discount on goods and services
  • Access to uncommon items for purchase
  • Temporary lodging provided when needed
  • Assistance with moderate challenges
3 (Trusted Allies)
  • 30% discount on goods and services
  • Access to rare items for purchase
  • Community will rally to defend characters
  • Specialized services offered freely
4 (Honored Heroes)
  • 50% discount on goods and services
  • Community adopts environmental practices
  • Community members may join as allies
  • Access to community resources and secrets
5 (Integral Members)
  • Honorary leadership position
  • Community becomes environmental stewards
  • Full access to all community resources
  • Community will mobilize for major causes
